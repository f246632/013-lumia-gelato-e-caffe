Welcome to Lumia

Experience the authentic taste of Italy in the heart of Berlin's vibrant Prenzlauer Berg neighborhood.

At Lumia Gelato e Caffé, we craft artisanal gelato using only the finest natural ingredients. Our commitment to quality means every scoop is 100% natural, with options for everyone including vegan, lactose-free, and gluten-free varieties.

Pair your gelato with our expertly prepared Italian coffee, made from premium beans and served by passionate baristas who understand the art of the perfect espresso.
